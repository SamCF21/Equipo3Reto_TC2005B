Story


Meet chef

In this adventure you will play as "chef", your own personalizable character that can improve over time. You may choose your weapon, clothes and your nationality. Chef is capable of attacking eneming and cooking its own food in order to defend the island. Choose wisely your upgrades and strategies as they will matter in the final battle.

It all starts when you arrive to an island, after investigatina a little you decide to start you empire of food sales. There you realize that the island is populated by the "fungi" a group of enemies that will try to destroy your business. Your mission is to use your chef skills to cook your food and defend yourself. With time, you will be able to get upgrades for your team, you will not be alone in this journey, your bother is there too ready to guide you. This is a strategy and RPG adventure.

How to play


Your choice matters

In this game you can choose between 3 nationalities, each one has its different food and advantages. Choose carefully as this will affect your game. You can choose between United States, Venezuela and Mexico.

To play use your mouse button, you can select the level (sand castle) you wish to play. There you can select where to put your soldiers (food) in the tile, find the best strategy to defend your food stall. Take into account that the level´s difficulty rises with each one you pass. Make sure to prepare yourself before entering a level. For that, choose wisely your upgrades in the upgrades section. There you can upgrade your chef, your food stall and your soldiers.
